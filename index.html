  • MGF of Exponential distribution

    • PDF of Exponential distribution`= f(x) = 1/theta e^(-x/theta)I_(x>0)`

    ‡¶è‡¶¨‡¶æ‡¶∞ ‡¶Ø‡¶¶‡¶ø ‡¶§‡ßã‡¶ï‡ßá ‡¶ï‡ßá‡¶â MGF ‡¶¨‡¶æ‡¶∞ ‡¶ï‡¶∞‡¶§‡ßá ‡¶¶‡ßá‡ßü ‡¶§‡¶æ‡¶π‡¶≤‡ßá ‡¶§‡ßÅ‡¶á at first ‡¶≤‡¶ø‡¶ñ‡¶¨‡¶ø

    MGF = `M_X(t) = E(e^(tX))`

    `=int_RRe^(tx)f(x)dx=int_RRe^(tx)1/theta e^(-x/theta)dx=int_0^ooe^(tx)/theta e^(-x/theta)dx`

    `=ubrace(int_0^ooe^(-x/theta(1-thetat))/thetadx)_("converges for " 1- thetat >0)`

    `=1/theta (Gamma1)/((1-thetat)/theta)=(1-thetat)^(-1)`

    `=1 + thetat + 2thetat^2/(2!) + 3(thetat)^3/(3!) + ..... + r(thetat)^r/(r!)`

    ‡¶è‡¶ü‡¶æ ‡¶Ø‡¶¶‡¶ø ‡¶®‡¶æ ‡¶¨‡ßÅ‡¶ù‡¶§‡ßá ‡¶™‡¶æ‡¶∞‡¶ø‡¶∏ ‡¶§‡¶æ‡¶π‡¶≤‡ßá ‡¶§‡ßã‡¶∞ HS ‡¶è‡¶∞ ‡¶≠‡¶ø‡¶§ ‡¶¶‡ßÅ‡¶∞‡ßç‡¶¨‡¶≤ ‡•§

    ‡¶è‡¶ü‡¶æ‡¶á ‡¶π‡¶ö‡ßç‡¶õ‡ßá MGF of Exponential distribution.

    Now, the coefficient of `t^r/(r!)` in the expansion of MGF is `= rtheta^r`

    So, rth order raw moment `mu_r^'=rtheta^r`

  • CGF of Exponential distribution

    • CGF = ln(MGF)

    `K_X(t)=ln(M_X(t))=-ln(1-thetat)`

    `=sum_(r=1)^oo(thetat)^r/r`

    `K_r=` coefficient of `t^r/(r!) " in the expansion of CGF "= theta^r(r-1)!`= rth order central moment (`mu_r`)

    `:.` 1st order central moment= mean = `theta`

    `:.` 2nd order central moment= variance = `theta^2`
